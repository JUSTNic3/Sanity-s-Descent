The Story

The player takes on a role of a computer scientist who has been working in an old office building for the last decade. Nothing ever changed. Day in, day out, everything is the same... until it isn't. Recently your bosses have started acting strange and the whole atmosphere is becoming eerie. Will you continue your normal everyday life or investigate the issue?

Be careful who you trust.
